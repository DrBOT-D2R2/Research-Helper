# Roadmap

## MVP

- Single-document and multi-document local ingestion
- Concept graph persisted in SQLite
- Search and learning-path generation
- Basic React Flow visualization

## PS-1 Version

- Graph editing and concept merging
- Better prerequisite inference and scoring
- Citation panels that point back to specific chunks
- Re-ingestion and partial refresh workflows
- Import/export of graph snapshots

## Future Version

- Local spaced repetition workflows
- Versioned graph history
- Pluggable extractors and alternative embedding models
- Multi-format knowledge dashboards
